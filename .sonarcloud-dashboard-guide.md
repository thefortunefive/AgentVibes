## If You Still Want to Change the Dashboard Settings

Here's a step-by-step guide to adjusting the quality gate threshold in SonarCloud:

### Step 1: Log into SonarCloud

1. Go to: https://sonarcloud.io/
2. Click "Log in" in the top right
3. Sign in with your GitHub account

### Step 2: Navigate to Your Project

1. After logging in, you should see your projects
2. Click on "**AgentVibes**" project
3. Or go directly to: https://sonarcloud.io/dashboard?id=paulpreibisch_AgentVibes

### Step 3: Go to Project Settings

1. Click the **"Administration"** tab at the top
2. From the dropdown, select **"Quality Gates"**
3. Or go directly to: https://sonarcloud.io/project/quality_gate?id=paulpreibisch_AgentVibes

### Step 4: Edit the Quality Gate

**Option A: Use a Different Quality Gate**
1. You'll see the current quality gate (probably "Sonar way")
2. Click **"Select another quality gate"**
3. Choose **"Sonar way (relaxed)"** which has lower thresholds
4. Click **"Use this quality gate"**

**Option B: Create a Custom Quality Gate**
1. Go to: https://sonarcloud.io/quality_gates
2. Click **"Create"**
3. Name it: "AgentVibes Custom"
4. Click **"Add Condition"**
5. Select: "Coverage on New Code"
6. Set: "is less than" → "90" (meaning fail if coverage is below 10%)
7. Click **"Add Condition"**
8. Go back to your project settings
9. Select your new "AgentVibes Custom" quality gate

### Step 5: Verify

1. Go back to: https://sonarcloud.io/dashboard?id=paulpreibisch_AgentVibes
2. The quality gate status should update after the next commit
